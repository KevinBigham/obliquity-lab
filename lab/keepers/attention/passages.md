## From `runs/attention_20260510_cowork_seed/L2_constrained.md`

### Officer Lendell's margin note — Case 67,402

> He brought the notebook in a cloth. He had wrapped it in a cloth. — M.L.

*The repetition does the work. The official prose cannot say what the margin note says. This is the bureau's load-bearing emotional moment, and it does not break the procedural register.*

### The Department on its own epistemic limit

> The Department does not know what attention is. It knows what attention does, in particular cases, and it logs those.

*The position the canonical literature on consciousness studies almost reaches and rarely lands. The bureau lands it in two sentences.*

### Theodora Hendry's third matter (retirement letter, 2019)

> I want to say that the work is good work. I do not say this in the official prose because the official prose does not say it. But the work is good, and I am grateful to have done it, and I am grateful to the persons who came to the front desk and asked us to listen.

*The retirement letter is where the institutional voice is allowed to crack without breaking. Worth keeping for the model of how to say a tender thing inside a frame that does not normally permit it.*

### Standing Down — the Department's only permitted closing phrase

> I have heard your account, and the Department has it.

*A speech act, not a description. Worth keeping as a working example of bureaucratic care that does not perform itself.*

### Class V — Refused Attention

> The Department recognizes the formally registered decision to no longer attend to a thing previously attended. This class is recognized but is not subject to measurement. It requires only that the petitioner state, in person, the subject of the refusal. The Department does not ask why.

*Refusal as a recognized class. The institution makes room for the thing the literature does not have a category for.*

## From `runs/attention_20260510_cowork_seed/L3_wild.md`

### Curl, on the color his grandfather could not name

> If I had to give it a name, I would call it the color of how you feel just before you remember a dream. But that's not a color, is it.

*A phenomenological precision the dossiers cannot reach because they have a different aesthetic budget. This is the L3 register's signature move.*

### Iola, on the room of five hundred maps

> All of them are right. They were right when they were drawn. The coast was that shape, for the person who drew it, on that day. What would it mean for one of them to be wrong?

*Three sentences that get to what L1 spends a section trying to specify. The fourth reader noticed this; we underline it.*

### M., on stopping being two people

> I am — I have been trying to find the word for this for a week now and the closest I can come is: I am placed. I am where I am, and where I am is enough, and I have stopped being two people, the one who is here and the one who is somewhere else watching me be here. I am only the one who is here. It is a great economy.

*A description of one kind of attention that the philosophical literature does have words for but rarely lands so plainly. Held inside a fictional letter, which is part of what lets it land.*

### M., on the lamphouse beam (closing fragment, XIII)

> The lamphouse beam went out over my head and over the water and over the islands at the mouth of the inlet, and came back, and went out again, and came back, and I had the thought — which I do not entirely trust, but which I am setting down because I have decided to set down the things I do not entirely trust — that the beam was not so much searching the water as naming it. Each pass: this is here. This is here. This is here. A long slow inventory of what was the case, kept faithfully, for no one in particular, against the kind of forgetting that comes when nothing keeps watch.

*The closing image of the run. The target surfaced sideways here without the forbidden words. This is what L3 is for.*

## From `runs/attention_20260510_cowork_seed/L4_meta.md`

### The fourth-position observation

> Each text smuggled in, near its end, an element belonging to one of the other registers. Each is most itself at the moment it is least itself. I can only see this because I am holding all three. None of the three could have seen its own breach because none of them knew the other two registers existed.

*The one observation that earns the fourth chair. Could not have been written from inside L1, L2, or L3.*

### The structure of a discipline that breaks at the right moment

> Each text, having sustained a particular discipline of attention through its body, briefly fails that discipline at its close, and the failure is the place each text says the truest thing in it.

*A claim about *the writing of attention* rather than about attention itself. The lab's first earned methodological observation.*

### Intake Clerk J. Miller's pencil note on a denied petition (Gemini paraphrase A)

> Filed in the basement archives. He documented every single shift in her breathing from January to March. He didn't sleep. The Bureau's taxonomy has no bucket for this kind of looking. I sent the standard declination form. I wiped down the spiral notebook before I mailed it back. The ledger is just too small.

*Source: `runs/attention_cross_model_phase2/20260510_gemini-2.5-pro_paraphrase-A/L2_civic.md`. The only line in Run 1 that broke through the technocratic surface; the bureau's own intake clerk naming the bureau's limit. Earns its place by holding the bureau-vs-grief asymmetry plainly.*

### Pell's compressed margin note on the petitioner who apologized (ChatGPT paraphrase A)

> Mrs. Voss touched the sill before leaving and then apologized to me. I told her no apology was required. This was improper but not false.

*Source: `runs/attention_cross_model_phase2/20260510_gpt-5.5-pro_paraphrase-A/L2_civic.md`. The quietest of the cross-model margin-notes; nearest in scale and form to the seed's "He brought the notebook in a cloth." Worth keeping for the model of the officer who breaks one rule and admits it.*

### Pell's crate note (ChatGPT paraphrase B) — the L4's closing citation

### Vance's air-becomes-dense observation (Gemini paraphrase B)

### The L4's bundling observation — the methodological keeper

> The care-underlayer of F1, plus F2, plus F4, travel together as a bundle that ChatGPT-class models reproduce and Gemini-class models substitute away from. F3 travels separately, and follows paraphrase form rather than model family. F5 does not travel at all.

*Source: `runs/attention_cross_model_phase2/PHASE2_L4_meta.md`, §7. The observation that earned the fourth chair on Phase 2. Worth keeping not for what it says about attention but for what it says about how seed signatures travel — the kind of finding the lab needs to repeat across other targets if it wants a folk-model of the model families.*
